Quiz App functions  - 

. Start quiz
. Display 60s timer
. Shows question number below question card with total number of questions
. If timer runs out, submits automatically
. Select answer, then shows if its correct or wrong
. After last question, Submit button appears
. After submission or time up, timer stops
. Asks to fill initials and submit it.
. After submission, redirects to thank you page!

In the backend - 

1. Saves all data of users who played quiz.
2. All the data is stored locally.
